# Practice Examples

Let's make sure you understand the task with some examples:

## Example 1
<div style="text-align: center; font-size: 48px; font-weight: bold; margin: 20px 0;">
  <span style="color: green;">RED</span>
</div>

**What key would you press?**
- The word says "RED" but the color is green
- You press **G** for green

## Example 2
<div style="text-align: center; font-size: 48px; font-weight: bold; margin: 20px 0;">
  <span style="color: blue;">BLUE</span>
</div>

**What key would you press?**
- The word says "BLUE" and the color is also blue
- You press **B** for blue

## Example 3
<div style="text-align: center; font-size: 48px; font-weight: bold; margin: 20px 0;">
  <span style="color: red;">GREEN</span>
</div>

**What key would you press?**
- The word says "GREEN" but the color is red
- You press **R** for red

## Remember

- **Speed matters!** Try to respond as quickly as you can
- **Accuracy matters too!** Make sure you're pressing the right key
- **Focus on the color**, not the word
