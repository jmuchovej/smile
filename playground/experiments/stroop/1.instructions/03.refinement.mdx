## Strategy Tips

1. **Position your fingers** on the R, G, and B keys before starting
2. **Focus your eyes** on the center of the screen
3. **Don't overthink** - your first instinct about the color is usually right
4. **Stay relaxed** - this task can be tricky but that's normal!

## What to Expect

- You'll see about **60-100 words**
- Each word appears for a **short time**
- Some will be **easy** (word and color match)
- Some will be **challenging** (word and color don't match)
- This is totally normal and expected!
