## Key Strategy

🎨 **Keep your eyes on the CENTER!** The flanking arrows are designed to distract you - that's the whole point of the experiment. Trust your focus on the middle arrow.

⚡ **Speed AND accuracy matter!** Try to respond as quickly as possible while staying correct.
