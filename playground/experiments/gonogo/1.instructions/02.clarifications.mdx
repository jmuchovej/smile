## Key Points

- **Most letters** will be "Go" letters (about 75% of the time)
- **Few letters** will be "No-Go" letters (about 25% of the time)
- **Speed matters** for Go letters - respond as fast as you can!
- **Self-control matters** for No-Go letters - resist the urge to press!
